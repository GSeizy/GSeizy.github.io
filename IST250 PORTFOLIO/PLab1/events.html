Annual Events in Zelienople

Our town is proud to host a variety of events throughout the year for residents and visitors alike.

★Miracle on Main Street★

When: Winter (Early December)

Zelienople's yearly Christmas celebration "Miracle on Main Street" is right around the corner! Come join us for this charity event which will feature Christmas floats, musical performances, horse & carraige rides, cookie decorating, an appearance by Santa himself, and so much more!

Horse Trading Days

When: Summer (Typically July)

This is our town's largest and most famous festival! For three days, Main Street comes alive with live music, over 100 food and craft vendors, a car cruise, and family-friendly entertainment.

Open Air Markets

When: Every Thursday (May-September)

Every Thursday from 5pm to 9pm during the May to September months, join us on main street for food, drink, and endless fun! Enjoy local shop discounts, craft products, samples, and boutique shopping to your heart's desire!
