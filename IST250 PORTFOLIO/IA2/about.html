About Vinyl Countdown

Our Story

Founded in 1991 by music enthusiast Miles Shelby, The Vinyl Countdown started as a small shop with a dream. Over our 30+ years in business, we have grown to be one of the largest record stores in the region

Welcome to The Vinyl Countdown, your one-stop destination for quality vinyl records! We've been happily serving music lovers with carefully curated collections spanning every genre imaginable for years now!

Our passion for music drives us in all we do! Doesn't matter if you are an experienced collector hunting for that rare record or an avid hobbyist just getting started, we're here to help you find exactly what you need.

We pride ourselves on a devoted staff who are always ready to share recommendations and stories about the albums they love. From classic rock to jazz, hip-hop to classical, and everything in-between, our inventory is sure to have something for everyone.

We believe that music is more than just entertainment. It's the very voice of generations! It gives us a link to our past, a soundtrack to today, and a legacy for the future music lovers. Every note tells a story, and we're grateful to help you write yours.

Visit Us Today!

Stop by our store to browse our collection, jam out, or even to just chat about music with like minded enthusiasts. We host vinyl swap meets here and there when the market is right and, occasionally, some live performances.
