Welcome Aspiring PC Builders!

My name is Jake and I started PCBuilderBase to give beginners the knowledge necessary to build their own PC from the ground up. Whether you are building for everyday use or for serious gaming, you've come to the right place!

So You Might Ask, Why Build Your Own PC?

Building your own PC might seem intimidating at first, but it is actually much easier than you think.
If you have some determination, patience, and a sprinkle of mechanical inclination, then you have come to the right place.

Here are some reasons why people choose to build their own:

  • Save $$$$ - You often get equal (or better) performance for less than pre-built costs.
  • Customization - With our guides, you can choose exactly what you need for your personal use case. You're not bound to what the builder or manufacturer provides.
  • Learn Some Useful Skills - Understanding your PC components helps with troubleshooting, understanding, and future upgrades/builds.
  • Easy to Upgrade - A few years go by and maybe your build isn't keeping up the way it used to, you'll know exactly what you need to do to get back on top.
  • Fun and Rewarding - From the first time you press the power button to everything you do after, there is a satisfaction knowing you built that yourself.
